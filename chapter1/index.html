My name is Dan Nguyen. My personal favorite drink of choice would be a honeydew smoothie. Ever since I first tried the bright green delicious concoction after school at Tapioca Express I fell in love with the sweet flavor. I had never had real honeydew until after and my sweet tooth self was disappointed with the fruit. The flavor of the honeydew in the smoothie had me hooked and has kept me trying this specific drink at every boba tea shop I can find.
