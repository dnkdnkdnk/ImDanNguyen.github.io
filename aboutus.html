Why We Serve

We are here to to take you on a wonderful excursion and help you enjoy your trip as much as possible. Our guides have lifelong experience being active and outdoors. They have been hiking for as long as they could run. They are native to the area that Pacific Trails is. Bright and beautiful souls, they will make sure you leave with new memories to cherish, and a new perspective on loving life.

Meet our wonderful guides!

Ricardo Montoya

Ricardo is a 28 year old native to this area. Everyone that has been graced by his pure and bright presence has said this about him "Caring and thoughtful, he will make sure you are the best experience possible while bringing pure joy through his enthusiasm and goofiness." He has been hiking for most of his life alongside his parents and friends. An expert swimmer and mastery of kayaks, he has encountered many different species of marinelife and aims to clean up any floating trash in the waters.

Caroline Esteban

Caroline is a 31 year old native to this area. Calm and kind, she will ensure that your time with us is relaxing and will ease you of all your worries. She is masterful at bringing people to a state of tranquility with her extensive knowledge of the Pacific Trails. Here is a personal account of one of our tour goers who had been going through tough times after losing their pet of 17 years: Caroline had allowed me to grieve in a way no one else could show me. After losing Fel I had a tough time appreciating the world around me. Caroline was able to bring a new light to my eyes and with her caring nature, she comforted and brought me to a peaceful state of mind.

Gain a new perspective!

Being in this enthusiastic environment will surely bring you closer to the passion that our guides exude. Lovers of their environment and nature, our tour guides will show you how to care for your surroundings and teach you how to respect your land.

Bring peace to your mind

The extraordinary views the pacific coast brings will allow to you appreciate the wondrous world that you live in. Beautiful views of sunsets and the ocean can provide you with core memories that you can tell your loved ones all about.

Leave feeling brand new!

Our goal is to ensure that you experience the local scenery and nature as a native would. To fully appreciate the land that provides life and joy to all around, these wonderful guides will allow you to implement this way of life into your own everyday experiences.
